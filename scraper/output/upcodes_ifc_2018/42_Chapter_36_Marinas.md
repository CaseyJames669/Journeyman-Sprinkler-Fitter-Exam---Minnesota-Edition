# Chapter 36 Marinas

About this chapter:Chapter 36 addresses the fire protection and prevention requirements formarinas. It was developed in response to the complications encountered by a number of fire departments responsible for the protection ofmarinasas well as fire loss history inmarinasthat lacked fire protection. Compliance with this chapter intends to establish safe practices inmarinaareas, provide an identification method for mooring spaces in themarina, and provide fire fighters with safe operational areas and fire protection methods to extend hose lines in a safe manner.

Plans formarinafire protectionfacilitiesshall beapprovedprior to installation. The work shall be subject to final inspection and approval after installation.
