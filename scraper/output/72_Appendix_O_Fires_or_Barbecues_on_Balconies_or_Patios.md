# Appendix O Fires or Barbecues on Balconies or Patios

The provisions contained in this appendix are not mandatory unless specifically referenced in the local adopting ordinance.

Exception:Listed electric or gas-fired barbecue grills that are permanently mounted and wired or plumbed to the building's gas supply or electricalsystemand that maintain a minimum clearance of 18 inches (457 mm) on all sides, unless listed for lesser clearances, may be installed on balconies and patios whenapprovedby thefire chief.
